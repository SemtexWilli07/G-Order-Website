How to order a bot?

1) Click on the big green button to start the ordering.

2) You will get redirected to a discord bot invite site from discord

3) Invite the bot to your own discord server (He needs only permissions to send and read messages)

4) Once the bot is on your server, type in /order

5) Congrats! The ordering process has started in your dms. Please answer to all questions
